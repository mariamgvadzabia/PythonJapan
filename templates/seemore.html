FEATURED • SECRETS • YOKOHAMA

Yokohama - the port of dreams

It is famous for its original "House-style" ramen, which features a thick, salty, pork-and-soy sauce broth

MUST-VISIT PLACES
Yokohama Chinatown • Minato Mirai 21 • Cup Noodle Museum
UNIQUE FACT
Yokohama is the birthplace of Japanese ice cream! In 1869, the first "Western-style" ice cream, called Aisu-kurin, was produced and sold in the Bashamichi district of Yokohama, marking the start of Japan's obsession with the dessert.

FEATURED • SECRETS • NARA

Nara - The Cradle of Tradition

Step back in time to Japan’s first permanent capital. Nara is a city where history breathes, filled with ancient temples that have stood for over 1,200 years and sacred nature that remains untouched.

MUST-VISIT PLACES
Nara Park • Todai-ji Temple (Great Buddha) • Kasuga Taisha Shrine
UNIQUE FACT
Nara's deer are national treasures! Over 1,200 wild Sika deer roam Nara Park freely. According to local legend, they were considered messengers of the gods. Today, they are so polite that they have learned to "bow" to visitors in exchange for special deer crackers (Shika-senbei)!
